About Anthropic

Anthropic’s mission is to create reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. We want AI to be safe and beneficial for our users and for society as a whole. Our team is a quickly growing group of committed researchers, engineers, policy experts, and business leaders working together to build beneficial AI systems.

About the Role

You will own the physical security posture of all Anthropic-spec data centers. Not managing guards and cameras—architecting defense-in-depth systems that scale from ground break through production, across multiple facilities, against sophisticated and well-resourced adversaries.

Our version controlled and AI assisted library act as one source of truth, driving consistent outputs and security that enables velocity rather than blocking it. Your job is to make it operational in steel, concrete, and sustained excellence.

Responsibilities: 

  • Physical Security Program Ownership- Define and execute the end-to-end physical security roadmap for all Anthropic data centers globally. 
  • Design and Engineering-  Ensure integrated physical security systems (access control, video surveillance, intrusion detection, perimeter security) that work across different types of facilities. Balance security requirements against operational efficiency and construction timelines.
  • Internal Partnership - Collaborate with Infrastructure, Operations, and Facilities teams to embed security into the data center lifecycle, from construction gates to steady-state operations.
  • Risk and Threat Modeling - Conduct risk assessments and threat modeling for greenfield and brownfield projects. Feed operational learnings back into the Controls framework. 
  • Compliance and Validation - Partner with Compliance and Legal to ensure physical security systems meet relevant standards,and customer requirements. 
  • Incident Response - Develop physical security incident response protocols with partners. When something happens, the team knows exactly what to do. 
  • Metrics and Accountability - Define KPIs that measure actual security posture, system effectiveness, vendor performance, incident metrics, coverage gaps. 

You may be a good fit if you:

  • Have worked with construction teams to integrate security into the build process
  • Can translate threat models into specific, measurable requirements
  • Have managed security programs across multiple facilities and geographies simultaneously
  • Know how to hold vendors accountable without destroying relationships
  • Can communicate security requirements to engineers, executives, lawyers, and construction managers and get buy-in from all of them
  • Are comfortable with ambiguity and can make decisions with incomplete information
  • Are energized by building from the ground up

Strong candidates may have:

  • 10+ years designing, deploying, and managing physical security for high-value compute infrastructure (data centers, critical infrastructure, sensitive facilities)
  • Hold relevant certifications (PSP, CPP, CISSP, CISM)

How we're different

We believe that the highest-impact AI research will be big science. At Anthropic we work as a single cohesive team on just a few large-scale research efforts. And we value impact — advancing our long-term goals of steerable, trustworthy AI — rather than work on smaller and more specific puzzles. We view AI research as an empirical science, which has as much in common with physics and biology as with traditional efforts in computer science. We're an extremely collaborative group, and we host frequent research discussions to ensure that we are pursuing the highest-impact work at any given time. As such, we greatly value communication skills.

The easiest way to understand our research directions is to read our recent research. This research continues many of the directions our team worked on prior to Anthropic, including: GPT-3, Circuit-Based Interpretability, Multimodal Neurons, Scaling Laws, AI & Compute, Concrete Problems in AI Safety, and Learning from Human Preferences.
