About the role

As a Sales Manager for Financials Services at Anthropic, you will be a key member of the French leadership team defining and executing on our FSI strategy. You'll lead a cross functional team including Enterprise Account Executives driving the adoption of safe, frontier AI by securing strategic deals with leading financial institutions across France. You'll leverage your leadership, consultative sales expertise and network in the Financial Services sector to propel revenue growth while developing a high-performing team 

Working closely with Applied AI Engineering and Product teams, you'll help Financial Services customers embed and deploy AI while uncovering its full range of capabilities across banking, insurance, asset management, and capital markets. In collaboration with GTM and Marketing teams, you'll continuously refine our value proposition, sales methodology, and market positioning to resonate with financial decision-makers and ensure differentiated value across the Financial Services landscape.

The ideal candidate is a hands-on leader  with a passion for developing people, identifying market opportunities within Financial Services, and executing strategies to capture them. By leading the deployment of Anthropic's emerging products, you will help financial institutions unlock new capabilities while also advancing the ethical development of AI.

Responsibilities:

  • Build and lead a high-performing team of Enterprise Account Executives focused on Financial Services, providing coaching, mentorship, and guidance to drive team success
  • Develop and implement sales strategies tailored to Financial Services procurement cycles and budgeting processes to meet and exceed team revenue quotas
  • Create and maintain accurate forecasts, pipeline management, and reporting while ensuring data integrity in CRM systems
  • Partner with Enablement to develop training programs and resources that accelerate AE ramp time and effectiveness, with specific focus on Financial Services buying cycles and decision-making processes
  • Work closely with Applied AI Engineering teams to demonstrate technical capabilities pre-sales and optimise solutions post-sales for financial applications
  • Enable your team to have credible technical conversations about AI applications in Financial Services while maintaining appropriate guardrails around compliance and regulatory considerations
  • Build strong partnerships across the organisation to drive strategic initiatives within the Financial Services sector
  • Identify and execute on new market opportunities and use cases for Anthropic's AI solutions within banking, insurance, asset management, and capital markets
  • Help your team navigate complex financial stakeholder ecosystems including executives, risk officers, compliance teams, IT departments, and procurement offices to build consensus
  • Own and nurture CxO relationships
  • Gather and synthesise customer feedback from Financial Services users to inform product direction and enhance customer experience
  • Build and continuously refine the Financial Services sales methodology by incorporating learnings into playbooks and best practices

You may be a good fit if you have:

  • 15+ years of enterprise sales experience in Financial Services technology, preferably driving adoption of SaaS or emerging technologies
  • 5+ years of sales leadership experience, ideally leading teams selling into Financial Services institutions in France or the broader European market
  • Proven track record of building and leading high-performing sales teams selling into financial institutions, with demonstrated success in coaching and developing AEs
  • Experience establishing and scaling sales processes, tools, and methodologies in fast-growing organisations serving Financial Services
  • Strong technical acumen and ability to understand complex AI solutions and their applications in Financial Services, with experience enabling teams to have credible technical conversations with stakeholders including CTOs, CDOs, and risk officers
  • Track record of successful partnership with technical teams throughout the customer journey within financial institutions
  • Deep understanding of Financial Services buying cycles, regulatory frameworks (including EU AI Act considerations), procurement processes, and key pain points
  • Demonstrated ability to navigate complex, multithreaded sales cycles across diverse stakeholder groups and drive cross-functional initiatives
  • Experience managing complex enterprise sales cycles within financial institutions and helping AEs secure strategic deals
  • Strong operational rigor in pipeline management, forecasting, and sales metrics specific to Financial Services sales cycles
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to build relationships across all levels, from analysts to C-suite executives in Financial Services
  • Fluency in French and English
  • Strategic approach to market analysis combined with tactical excellence in execution
  • Passion for AI technology and commitment to its safe, responsible development for financial use cases

How we're different

We believe that the highest-impact AI research will be big science. At Anthropic we work as a single cohesive team on just a few large-scale research efforts. And we value impact — advancing our long-term goals of steerable, trustworthy AI — rather than work on smaller and more specific puzzles. We view AI research as an empirical science, which has as much in common with physics and biology as with traditional efforts in computer science. We're an extremely collaborative group, and we host frequent research discussions to ensure that we are pursuing the highest-impact work at any given time. As such, we greatly value communication skills.

The easiest way to understand our research directions is to read our recent research. This research continues many of the directions our team worked on prior to Anthropic, including: GPT-3, Circuit-Based Interpretability, Multimodal Neurons, Scaling Laws, AI & Compute, Concrete Problems in AI Safety, and Learning from Human Preferences.
