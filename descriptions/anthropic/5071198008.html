About the Role

We're hiring our first dedicated international tax professional in Europe, based in Dublin. You'll be a key member of Anthropic's tax team, working closely with our Head of International Tax Operations and Business Advisory and reporting into our San Francisco headquarters.

As European Tax Lead, you'll own direct tax compliance and advisory across our European entities (Ireland, UK, Germany, France, Switzerland) and help build tax infrastructure to support Anthropic's rapid international expansion.

Responsibilities:

  • Lead European direct tax compliance, working with external advisors (KPMG, PwC, Deloitte) to ensure timely and accurate filings across all jurisdictions
  • Support transfer pricing documentation and intercompany transactions for European entities, including cost-plus arrangements and IP licensing structures
  • Advise on permanent establishment risks, entity structuring, and operational tax considerations as Anthropic expands European commercial activities
  • Support EU AI Act-related entity and operational structuring from a tax perspective
  • Partner with indirect tax personnel on VAT/GST matters to align on tax strategy and overall compliance requirements.
  • Partner with Finance, Legal, and People teams on European payroll taxes, equity compensation, and employee mobility matters
  • Build relationships with local tax authorities and manage any inquiries or audits
  • Contribute to global tax projects including public company readiness, SOX compliance, and tax technology implementations

You may be a good fit if you:

  • 10–12 years of international tax experience, ideally with a mix of Big 4/advisory and in-house roles
  • Strong technical knowledge of Irish corporate tax, EU tax directives, and cross-border tax issues
  • Deep experience with transfer pricing documentation, intercompany agreements, and IP planning structures
  • Exposure to technology or high-growth company tax matters preferred
  • CTA, AITI, or equivalent professional qualification
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to work across time zones with US-based colleagues
  • Self-starter who thrives in ambiguity and can build processes from the ground up

Strong candidates may have:

  • Experience with R&D tax credits and knowledge-based capital regimes
  • Knowledge of Pillar Two/global minimum tax rules and digital services taxes
  • Experience supporting IPO readiness or public company tax compliance

How we're different

We believe that the highest-impact AI research will be big science. At Anthropic we work as a single cohesive team on just a few large-scale research efforts. And we value impact — advancing our long-term goals of steerable, trustworthy AI — rather than work on smaller and more specific puzzles. We view AI research as an empirical science, which has as much in common with physics and biology as with traditional efforts in computer science. We're an extremely collaborative group, and we host frequent research discussions to ensure that we are pursuing the highest-impact work at any given time. As such, we greatly value communication skills.

The easiest way to understand our research directions is to read our recent research. This research continues many of the directions our team worked on prior to Anthropic, including: GPT-3, Circuit-Based Interpretability, Multimodal Neurons, Scaling Laws, AI & Compute, Concrete Problems in AI Safety, and Learning from Human Preferences.
