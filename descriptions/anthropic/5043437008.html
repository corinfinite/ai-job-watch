About Anthropic

Anthropic’s mission is to create reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. We want AI to be safe and beneficial for our users and for society as a whole. Our team is a quickly growing group of committed researchers, engineers, policy experts, and business leaders working together to build beneficial AI systems.

About the role

Anthropic is seeking a Global Technology Partner Lead to own and scale our strategic partnerships with leading technology platforms. In this role, you will be the architect of multi-dimensional relationships spanning product integration, co-selling, channel enablement, and joint go-to-market execution. You will be accountable for transforming these partnerships into major revenue drivers while ensuring the partners capture significant value from the rapidly evolving AI landscape.

This is a high-impact role requiring someone who can operate at the executive level with partner leadership while simultaneously driving tactical wins across priority accounts. You will own the strategic roadmap, investment decisions, and partnership-influenced revenue accountability for what will become some of Anthropic's most important technology partnerships.

Responsibilities

  • Serve as the strategic owner of Global Technology Partner relationships, driving sell-with (joint selling to enterprise accounts) and sell-through (partner-led channel sales) motions, while partnering closely with Anthropic's sales team on sell-to opportunities where these partners are customers
  • Define partnership investment strategy, market and vertical prioritization, and overall architecture for mutual value creation
  • Support Anthropic's sales team in pursuit of significant deals with technology partners, providing partnership context, executive relationships, and strategic alignment to accelerate deal closure
  • Build and maintain executive relationships within partner's leadership team, identifying and influencing their largest transformation opportunities
  • Drive product integration roadmaps in collaboration with Anthropic's product and engineering teams, ensuring Claude is deeply embedded in partner platforms
  • Develop competitive differentiation messaging and comprehensive enablement programs across partner sales and technical teams
  • Create sales tools, training curricula, and certification programs that equip partner professionals to effectively position Claude
  • Partner with technical teams to build production-grade solutions powered by Claude, including reference architectures and industry-specific accelerators
  • Establish success criteria for joint accounts, ensure proper implementation support, and create feedback loops for continuous improvement
  • Track and report on partnership-influenced revenue contribution, ensuring partners are achieving their goals while collaborating with sales leadership on sell-to pipeline and forecasting

You may be a good fit if you

  • Have 6+ years of experience managing strategic partnerships with leading data or cloud platforms
  • Possess existing VP+ level relationships within technology partner leadership
  • Have previously owned partnerships generating $50M+ in influenced revenue
  • Have supported or partnered on enterprise deals exceeding $5M in contract value, providing strategic partnership leverage to accelerate outcomes
  • Understand the mechanics of complex, multi-stakeholder enterprise sales cycles and can engage effectively with C-suite buyers
  • Have experience navigating technical product integrations and can partner effectively with engineering teams on API integrations and platform capabilities
  • Have built partnerships in high-growth technology categories where partner enablement, competitive positioning, and technical differentiation were critical
  • Have operated successfully in roles requiring coordination across multiple internal teams and external stakeholder groups
  • Understand P&L dynamics, forecasting, and channel partnership economics, with experience influencing revenue through partnership motions
  • Combine strategic vision with execution discipline—equally comfortable presenting to executives and working directly on deal strategy

Strong candidates may also

  • Have deep familiarity with the AI and data platform landscape and can articulate Claude's technical and philosophical differentiation
  • Bring experience building partnership programs, metrics, and operating cadences from scratch
  • Have a track record of creating structure while maintaining speed and avoiding bureaucracy
  • Demonstrate intellectual rigor in partnership strategy paired with pragmatism in execution
  • Have technical fluency with data infrastructure concepts, lakehouse architectures, and enterprise data governance

How we're different

We believe that the highest-impact AI research will be big science. At Anthropic we work as a single cohesive team on just a few large-scale research efforts. And we value impact — advancing our long-term goals of steerable, trustworthy AI — rather than work on smaller and more specific puzzles. We view AI research as an empirical science, which has as much in common with physics and biology as with traditional efforts in computer science. We're an extremely collaborative group, and we host frequent research discussions to ensure that we are pursuing the highest-impact work at any given time. As such, we greatly value communication skills.

The easiest way to understand our research directions is to read our recent research. This research continues many of the directions our team worked on prior to Anthropic, including: GPT-3, Circuit-Based Interpretability, Multimodal Neurons, Scaling Laws, AI & Compute, Concrete Problems in AI Safety, and Learning from Human Preferences.
